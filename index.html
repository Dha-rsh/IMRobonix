Robotic functionality in a handheld minimally invasive surgical device

Redefine Surgical Innovation with Advanced Robotic Technology

SurgiKot is a lightweight, high-precision handheld smart medical robotic device designed for multifunctional surgical procedures. It features an articulating end gripper and advanced laparoscopic instruments, ensuring accuracy in operations. CheriBot, a smart robotic bionic upper limb, offers high grasping capability, allowing amputees to mimic human hand movements with 15 degrees of freedom. Utilizing camera vision, it adapts to various contours for enhanced control and functionality.
